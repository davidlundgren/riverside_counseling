Professional Experience

Pamela has dedicated twenty-six years to providing therapeutic services to clients with varying concerns. These efforts at healing, and rejuvenating have taken her to three different states and  back home to the Catskills in rural Sullivan County.

Pamela continues to be enthusiatic and hopeful about the unlimited potential which her clients possess.  

 Degrees and certifications include:

  • B.S., Special Education/Psychology
    • Syracuse University, New York
  • M.A., Psychology/Drama Therapy
    • Antioch University
    • California Institute of Integral Studies
  • NYS Licensed Mental Health Counselor
  • NYS Certification, School Psychologist
  • EMDR Level II Certification

Family Services/EAP

Pamela has spent twenty-one years providing assessment, consultation, treatment and referral for children, adolescents, adults and families in medical centers, community mental health agencies and schools. Pamela also worked in a large corporation under the auspices of public health performing employee assistance services (EAP), including managed care and case management for contracted clinicians. 

Addiction Counseling 

Pamela worked for five years directly in the field of chemical dependency treatment.  During this time, she designed and implemented a family education and drama therapy program with adults in treatment, provided therapy for adult children of alcoholics and published a thesis on the recovery work of these clients as part of an original research project. Certified Substance Abuse Counselor for ten years. 

Business Experience

Pamela worked for five years    in marketing.
