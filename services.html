Services

Since I have varied and extensive professional experiences, I am able to offer in depth work in several key areas.  These include but are not limited to EAP (Employee Assistance Program Services), Child, Adolescent, Adult and Family Services, Counseling for Addictions and related issues, Solutions to Core Issues in Recovery, Grief Counseling, EMDR work to resolve trauma and relieve emotional distress, Personal growth and creativity enhancement, Sexuality/Intimacy issues and health concerns.

EAP Work

Provide training on use of counseling services for large and small businesses. Affliate provider of direct counseling services for EAP employees and family members.  Resource and referral service for EAP members.

EMDR

Use of a physiologically based therapy to relieve many types of psychological distress.  Also employed to assist in recovery from addictions, and creativity enhancement.  

Addiction Counseling/Related Issues

Individual, family and group therapy available.  Adult Child of Alcoholic Recovery Protocol. Resource and referral/12 Step orientation.   

Family Therapy

Use of family sculpting, role-play, psychodrama, other creative arts techniques employed in addition to talk sessions. 

Bereavement/Grief Counseling

Supportive, healing individual, family and group work offered.

Personal Coaching

Self-esteem concerns, life purpose/transitions/creativity enhancement and personal spiritual beliefs explored. Relaxation/meditative exercises.
