Welcome to Riverside Counseling!

Pamela Lundgren is pleased to announce the start of her private practice in Sullivan County in the town of Callicoon.  A native daughter of the area, Pamela shares her expertise, professional experience and hope with others in her counseling. The goal is for clients to receive relief from painful experiences, attain clarity and understanding, have healthy and meaningful relationships, and to experience joy, peace and serenity in their lives.

Various treatment modalities are employed depending upon the needs of the clients. Counseling may include cognitive behavioral techniques, (thought, feeling, belief), transpersonal psychology (meditation, consciousness, spiritual connections) and creative arts therapy (drama therapy, relationship sculpting and psychodrama).

Pamela has excellent professional relationships with doctors and health practitioners in the community.  These professionals offer conventional as well as alternative and complementary therapies. Consultation with others is encouraged to assist in healing the whole person and to offer preventative paths to maintain a happy and healthy life style.
